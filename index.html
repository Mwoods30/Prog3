Lekagul Traffic Atlas

A focused investigation dashboard for the Lekagul Preserve sensor records. It combines the raw CSV with findings from the two progress decks, then turns the traffic logs into a clear story about unusual restricted access, possible dumping activity, and the baseline patterns that make those anomalies stand out.

Main Argument The strongest evidence points to repeated non-ranger activity near restricted gates and Ranger-Stop 3, especially during the Tuesday/Thursday overnight window. The dashboard is organized so you can move from summary findings, to traffic patterns, to the exact map locations supporting that claim.
